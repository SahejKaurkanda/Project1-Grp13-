Contact Us

Reach out to us for any inquiries.


MDN Web Docs Feedback: You can provide feedback directly on the MDN website through the feedback option available on most pages. This allows you to report issues, suggest improvements, or ask questions about specific content.

MDN Web Docs Community: MDN has an active community of developers who contribute to the platform and help each other through forums and discussions. You can engage with the community by participating in discussions, asking questions, or sharing your knowledge.

Social Media: You can follow MDN on social media platforms such as Twitter (@MozDevNet) or join Mozilla-related groups and communities on platforms like Reddit or GitHub.

Mozilla Support: While not specific to MDN, Mozilla has a general support page where you can find help with Mozilla products, including developer tools and resources. You can visit the Mozilla Support page at https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/.

Bug Reporting: If you encounter technical issues or bugs on the MDN website itself, you can report them through Mozilla's Bugzilla platform at https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/.
